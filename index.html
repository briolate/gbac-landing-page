Re-open your business confidently with the GBAC STAR™ Facility Accreditation Program. It’s the cleaning industry’s leading third-party accreditation for cleaner and safer facilities.

GBAC STAR is a management standard that helps facilities and front-line staff establish a comprehensive system of cleaning, disinfection, and infectious disease prevention. GBAC STAR is for businesses of any size that want to re-open safely following the coronavirus pandemic.
